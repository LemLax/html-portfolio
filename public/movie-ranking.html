The Best Movies According to Lem

My top 3 movies of all time

Fury

This is one of my favorite movies of all time. Shows how it's like as a tanker soldier during WWII.
This movie show the camaraderie of the soldiers and the horrific events that happened during that time.
The acting, special effects and story are amazing.

Fast and Furious Tokyo Drift

Really cool movie about Japananese cars drifting.

Demon Slayer Mugen Train

This is my favourite anime movie. The battles are very cool and the story is awesome.
